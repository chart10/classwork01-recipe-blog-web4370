Overnight Blueberry Chia Pudding


This delicious blueberry chia seed pudding is such a simple summertime breakfast dish if you can get your hands on sweet, fresh blueberries. But even frozen blueberries work great if you're looking to make this treat any time of year where fresh blueberries aren't so easy to find. You can also substitute chopped stawberries or blackberries to mix things up. Get creative!

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup Fresh or Frozen Blueberries
  • 1 1/2 cups Silk Unsweetened Almondmilk or Oatmilk
  • 1/2 cup Greek Yogurt
  • 2 Tbsp Maple Syrup
  • 1 tsp Vanilla
  • A Pinch of Cinnamon (optional)

Directions:

Combine all the ingredients in a container and store in the fridge overnight. Top with fresh fruit, granola, or chopped nuts.
